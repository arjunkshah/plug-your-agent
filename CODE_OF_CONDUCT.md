# Code of Conduct

We are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free
experience for everyone. We expect all participants to be considerate and
professional.

## Our standards

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent.
- Focus on what is best for the community.

## Enforcement

Unacceptable behavior may be reported by opening an issue or contacting the
maintainers. Reports will be reviewed and acted upon promptly.
